## OUR GOALS

Here we discuss various security & vulnerabilities of web application, API, Mobile & other devices. We share tools and techniques related to web application security. The main aim of creating this community is to bring all the cyber security expert together. So let’s get together and move for securing web applications.
We aim at providing exposure to our regional cyber experts by organizing informative talks, sessions, boot-camps and so on.
